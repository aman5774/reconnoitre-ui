OUR PROCESS

  1. Register yourself.
  2. Login to your account.
  3. Go to Update Profile section on the dashboard and upload your Photograph,resume,marksheets and certificates.Also fill the personal and academic details.(Fill & Upload carefully because all the evaluations will be done on that.You need not to carry resume and other Stuffs.)
  4. If already upadated the profile section then go to Event registration module.
  5. In Event Registration module,provide event id and corresponding college list will be generated.Select the college you belong to.
  6. On the event day,come with your one Identity proof document,no need to carry your resume and other stuff as you have already uploaded on our website.
  7. Attend the pre-placement talk and then proceed to the online test phase.
  8. Go to Online test module on dashboard provide the Event ID and access code will be given there by our representative on the spot.Start Test.
  9. After clearing the test, your interviews will be done accordingly.

ABOUT US

Reconnoitre is India's largest assessment and hiring platform. We run assessments to measure employability across all domains - from programming to plumbing. Reconnoitre works with 50+ corporate clients to execute entry level and lateral assessments and has access to more than a million candidates through its platform. We run 3 million+ assessments each year in 50+ cities; thus, everyday helping our corporate clients reduce interview bandwidth using our central assessment platform, helping our institutional clients measure and improve employability and helping candidates move ahead on their career path. Our vision is to ensure that everyone in the country gets an equal opportunity to build a career. We are the visionaries of this space with a strong growth trajectory and an exciting firm to be with!
